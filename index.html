Approach

OpenCivix is designed to support monitoring, documentation, and structured review of public-facing digital activity relevant to election operations.

The platform emphasizes transparency, traceability, and standardized reporting workflows rather than directing or influencing outcomes.

It is intended for use by institutions requiring visibility into public digital signals and related operational context.
